# Hardware Requirements

Complete hardware specifications for Physical AI course.

## Simulation Workstation (Required)

**GPU:** NVIDIA RTX 4070 Ti (12GB VRAM) or higher
**CPU:** Intel i7 13th Gen or AMD Ryzen 9  
**RAM:** 64GB DDR5 (32GB minimum)
**OS:** Ubuntu 22.04 LTS

## Edge AI Kit (Recommended)

**Brain:** NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano (8GB)
**Eyes:** Intel RealSense D435i
**Cost:**$700-$1000

## Robot Platforms

**Budget:** Unitree Go2 (~$3,000)
**Premium:** Unitree G1 Humanoid (~$16,000)
